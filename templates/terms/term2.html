2nd Term

Competencia:

INTERACTUAR EN LENGUA INGLESA DE FORMA ORAL Y ESCRITA DENTRO DE CONTEXTOS SOCIALES Y LABORALES SEGÚN LOS CRITERIOS ESTABLECIDOS POR EL MARCO COMÚN EUROPEO DE REFERENCIA PARA LAS LENGUAS.

Resultado de aprendizaje:

INTERCAMBIAR OPINIONES SOBRE SITUACIONES COTIDIANAS Y LABORALES ACTUALES, PASADAS Y FUTURAS EN CONTEXTOS SOCIALES ORALES Y ESCRITOS.

Main Topic

Describing what is happening right now and talking about any current trend.

Learning goals

  • Can describe his/her uniform characteristics (material, color, patterns).
  • Can talk about actions happening at the moment of speaking.
  • Can listen to people talking about actions in progress.
  • Can describe what people are wearing.

Main Topic

Talking about memories and last experiences, giving opinons about past events

Learning goals

  • Can recall past events.
  • Can describe important past states.
  • Can ask people about past feelings and states.
  • Can listen to people describing specific past states.

Main Topic

Exchanging information about anecdotes and people's background. Describing important deeds from the past time

Learning goals

  • Can describe people's influence in history.
  • Can use a variety of vocabulary to describe the past tense.
  • Can ask people for specifications.
  • Can understand videos describing people's influence in history.
  • Can listen to people describing specific past states.

Main Topic

Using a wide variety of vocabulary to make comparisons.

Learning goals

  • Can identify some phonemes when pronouncing adjectives.
  • Can use adjectives to compare.
  • Can use adjectives to establish differences between two people, two objects and two places.
  • Can elicit information about comparisons.

Main Topic

Indicating the characteristics of different elements and making comparisons among them.

Learning goals

  • Can compare things, places and people.
  • Can describe places, objects and people's characteristics.
  • Can elicit information from texts about comparisons and descriptions.
  • Can understand conversations about comparisons and descriptions.

Main Topic

Progress check and mid term, Review. GRAMMAR APPROACH

Main Topic

Asking for and giving suggestions, requests and advice. Setting permissions and obligations for general contexts. Talking in formal and informal ways.

Learning goals

  • Can give advice to other students for solving academic and family problems.
  • Can talk about rules and prohibitions.
  • Can understand different types of signs and symbols related to permission and prohibition.
  • Can listen to people talking about rules and prohibitions.

Main Topic

Making invitations and expressing personal opinions, likes, dislikes, excuses and needs.

Learning goals

  • Can talk about preferences and desires.
  • Can ask people about their preferences.
  • Can write about preferences and desires related to academic and labour issues.
  • Can listen to people talking about preferences and desires.
  • Can listen to people talking about rules and prohibitions.

Main Topic

Expressing spontenaous decisions and predictions. Making promisses about the future

Learning goals

  • Can make predictions about the future.
  • Can talk about possible future events.
  • Can ask people about their opinions and predictions for the future.
  • Can elicit information about future possible events.

Main Topic

Making plans and invitations, accepting and refusing challenges, dates, appoinments, and general requests.

Learning goals

  • Can talk about intentions and plans.
  • Can ask people about their plans and intentions.
  • Can listen to people talking about their intentions and plans.
  • Can understand basic questions about plans and intentions.

Main Topic

Review, check evidences, heteroevaluation, final evaluation, giving notes, last updates, catching up, etc
