5th Term

Competencia:

INTERACTUAR EN LENGUA INGLESA DE FORMA ORAL Y ESCRITA DENTRO DE CONTEXTOS SOCIALES Y LABORALES SEGÚN LOS CRITERIOS ESTABLECIDOS POR EL MARCO COMÚN EUROPEO DE REFERENCIA PARA LAS LENGUAS.

Resultado de aprendizaje:

DISCUTIR SOBRE POSIBLES SOLUCIONES A PROBLEMAS DENTRO DE UN RANGO VARIADO DE CONTEXTOS SOCIALES Y LABORALES.

Main Topic

Making questions to confirm information and expressing personal opinion in a more polite way. Making offers and invitations.

Learning goals

  • Can ask people about their present and past most relevant aspects.
  • Can ask for clarification in a polite way.
  • Can express himself/herself using formal expressions.
  • Can request for favors or any specific information from classmates.

Main Topic

Asking for help, requesting information in a formal and appropriate mood, being more polite with people.

Learning goals

  • Can ask for directions in the city in a formal way.
  • Can ask and answer instructions for simple processes in the classroom.
  • Can understand people giving directions.
  • Can exchange opinions about specific topics in class.

Main Topic

Telling stories and narrating sequencing events. Explaining steps of different processes.

Learning goals

  • Can describe processes (recipes) in sequence.
  • Can give specific details when explaining a process.
  • Can elicit information from texts on processes descriptions.
  • Can ask people about the steps of different procedures.

Main Topic

Talking about execution of duties. Evaluating service performance and describing how actions take place.

Learning goals

  • Can describe changes in specific activities.
  • Can compare changes when describing past and present events.
  • Can describe specific details when comparing situations.
  • Can exchange information about classmates’ behavior changes.

Main Topic

Expressing degree of certaintity in diverse situations. Giving your opinion with hesitation.

Learning goals

  • Can infer the possible result of different specific situations.
  • Can deduct the result of a scientific experiment in class.
  • Can give opinions about classmates behavior.
  • Can express different levels of certainty.

Main Topic

Progress check and mid term, Review. GRAMMAR APPROACH

Main Topic

Discussing about preferences according to different lifestyles and andgiving instructions about the use and purpose of diverse devices.

Learning goals

  • Can talk about leisure activities and preferences.
  • Can ask and answer questions about likes and dislikes.
  • Can understand people describing likes and dislikes.
  • Can use a considerable range of expressions for describing preferences.

Main Topic

Describing with detail positive and negative features of people, places and situations. Showing your own perception of things.

Learning goals

  • Can give specific details of technical items and processes.
  • Can exchange opinions when describing devices or technical processes.
  • Can understand simple object descriptions.
  • Can give positive and negative opinions about specific situations.

Main Topic

Expanding vocabulary and using your own background knowledge to predict and discuss events of everyday life.

Learning goals

  • Can talk about the meaning of specific symbols and concepts.
  • Can exchange information about concepts and symbols understanding.
  • Can understand short texts explaining concepts or definitions.
  • Can use different suffixes and prefixes properly.

Main Topic

Asking for and giving reasons. Discussing the causes and consequences of different events.

Learning goals

  • Can talk about the consequences of specific situations.
  • Can connect his/her ideas with a great variety of words.
  • Can exchange opinions when talking about the results of specific situations.
  • Can write a paragraph explaining the results and consequences of specific situations.

Main Topic

Review, check evidences, heteroevaluation, final evaluation, giving notes, last updates, catching up, etc
